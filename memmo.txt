MEMORANDUM OF DEMANDS
TO WHOM IT MAY CONCERN
We, the undersigned citizens and residents, hereby submit this memorandum to draw the urgent attention of the concerned authorities towards the pressing issues affecting our community. We respectfully urge the immediate and decisive action on the following demands:

Immediate repair and maintenance of all public roads and infrastructure within the municipality, with a timeline of 90 days and regular progress updates.
Establishment of a reliable and uninterrupted 24×7 drinking water supply system, ensuring quality testing and distribution to all wards equally.
Installation of adequate street lighting in all residential areas and public spaces to enhance safety and security for citizens.
Setting up a functioning and well-equipped primary health centre in underserved localities, staffed with qualified medical professionals.
Proper solid waste management, including regular door-to-door collection, scientific disposal, and implementation of wet/dry waste segregation norms.
We, the signatories listed below, hereby affirm that all information provided is true and correct to the best of our knowledge. We trust that the authorities will consider these demands with the seriousness they deserve and take prompt remedial action in the public interest.
